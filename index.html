Essentials

A complete set of tools for Autodesk® Revit® designed to improve coordination, refine system connections, simplify family and system management, and increase model accuracy.

Sheet Bridge

Easily export and import Schedules, View Lists, Sheet Lists, and Key Schedules to and from Excel. Use Excel's capabilities for efficient data management and bring updates back into your project with ease.

Intersect

Aligns pipes or ducts with elevation conflicts for easier manual connections, particularly useful in sloped sanitary systems.

Leapfrog

Quickly resolve clashes by allowing pipes, ducts, cable trays, and conduits to jump over or under obstacles with precise, user-defined offsets and angles.

View Tools

Manage element visibility by identifying hidden elements, pinning or unpinning views and restoring manually hidden elements.
